Chicken Casserole!

Chicken & Stuffing Casserole Courtesy of My Mom

This simple recipe is delicious and smells great while cooking. A hit with company!

Ingredients:

  • 4 whole chicken breasts (8 halves), boned and skinned
  • 1 can of cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 can of cream of celery soup
  • 1 cup of sour cream
  • 1 cup of melted butter
  • 1 cup of chicken broth
  • 1 pkg. Pepperridge Farm Fine Herb Stuffing

How To Make It!

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Mix soups and sour cream together
  3. Place chicken in baking dish and coat well with mixture
  4. Top with 1 pkg. Pepperridge Farm Fine Herb Stuffing (not cubed)
  5. Melt and pour 1 cup of melted butter over chicken
  6. Pour 1 cup of chicken broth over chicken
  7. Bake for 1 hour uncovered
  8. Serve with white rice or noodles!
