Deviled Eggs!

Quick And Easy Deviled Eggs Courtesy of My Mom

Deviled Eggs are a flexible and fun food that can wear many hats. From a quick and easy way to satiate guests arriving to a dinner party, to a nutritious and tasty breakfast, deviled eggs are a great all around dish from yesteryear often forgotten about these days. Below you find a simple recipe that will taste great! There are many ways to make this your own and add your own twang to it!

Ingredients:

  • Eggs
  • Mayo
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Mustard
  • Paprika
Optional:
  • Olives
  • Jalepeno Slice
  • Hot Sauce, etc!

    • How To Make It!

    1. Boil Eggs-Cover eggs with water and once water is boiling cook for 10 minutes
    2. Take eggs out and put in an icebath for 10 minutes
    3. Peel eggs and slice in half
    4. Remove the yolks and put into a bowl
    5. Smash the yolks with a fork
    6. Add mayo, salt, pepper and a shot of mustard and mix together
    7. Fills the egg white with the mixture you made and top with paprika and anything else you think would be good!
